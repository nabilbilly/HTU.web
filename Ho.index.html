HISTORY OF HO TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY

The origin ofHo Technical University goes back to 1968. It was established as Technical institute with a primary objective of providing pre-technical education .By 1972, the institution had made tremendous program in upgradind of the course offered. Consequantly the pre-technical program expanded to course in Engineering and vocational field, e.g

  • Basic Engineering
  • Agricultural and Machanical Engineering
  • Building technology
  • Fashion
  • Hospitality Management and Business

in 1986, the institute became a polytechnic.it however continued to operat as circular institute,until 1973 the polytechnic was upgraded to a full-flaged tertiary institute by the enactment
of[PNDC] Law 321 and changed with the responsibility of trainning students in the technical and the vocational skill to the Higher National Diploma[HND] LEVEL . Substequently in 2007,polytechnics where given th mandate byPolytechnics Act 745 to win their own degree to the heighest level.
The passage of Technical University Act 2016[Act 922] provide Ho Technical University and mandated it to award degree,diploma, certificate and other qualifications to the heighest level in Engineering, Science, and Technology based deciplines.
